Maxwell Ohiorenoya Park-Ojota ,Lagos-Nigeria

My travel tour to Maxwell Ohiorenoya park in Ojota
Statue & park dedicated to an international activist of repute & defender of Rights and justice.


This is a symbolic park named after the iconic legend, Chief Maxwell Ohiorenoya. A Lawyer per excellence and an advocate for the common man,
Today , the park service is a melting pot for all struggles emanating in the mega city (Lagos).In 2010, a park in Ojota was named after him by the Lagos state government.
